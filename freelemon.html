PROTO PERSONA

USER INTERVIEW

Background

Our team is developing a website that allows people with excess food (either grown themselves or purchased) to donate to others in their local community. Community members are able to collect the food for free or donate a small amount if they are willing and able to. Our aim with this website is to reduce food waste by allowing people to donate to others in their local area and increase community involvement.

Goals

To understand who a typical user of the platform might be, and then to empathise with what they might need and want out of the user experience. To understand what users might be looking for in a potential solution, and to define functional areas to prioritise in the development.

Research Questions

1.How aware are people of the level of food wastage in their household?
2.How can we encourage people to have food wastage prevention in their community?
3.How do people currently try to reduce food waste?
4.How do people currently work towards sustainability?

Methodology

1-on-1 semi-structured interview over Zoom (5-10 minutes per participant) • Questionnaire Online survey posted on social media channels.

Participants

5 x participants
Survey participants over 3-day period

USER INTERVIEW FINDINGS

“I throw out food almost every day.”

“Eating local produce is very important to me.”

“I try to eat leftovers within 1-2 days, and then have to discard it.”

"I don’t really feel connected to my community, but I would like to.”

SURVEY & FINDINGS

To better analyse the food waste patterns within our user group, we distributed a survey to gather quantitative data for a targeted development approach.

The survey yield some strong and correlating responses to the qualitative research we undertook.

83% would like to give back to the community.
48% throw out food every day.
87% would take part in local initiative to reduce food waste.

PROBLEM STATEMENT & USER INSIGHT STATEMENT

Problem Stament

We believe that creating a convenient way for socially-aware people to swap or donate excess fresh produce to others in the community will reduce the amount of food waste in the community.

User Insight Statement

Socially-conscious, time-poor people need to find a more convenient way to reduce food waste through donating to others in the community. Users are feeling increasingly guilty about wasting food regularly and need a simple solution to the problem.

4 Final Thoughts


  • Keeping accessibility front of mind while designing can be really challenging and you will likely have to make design aesthetic sacrifices to make it work.
  • Sometimes the most difficult part of designing is putting yourself in the position of the user and not relying solely on user testing & interviews.
  • Testing is so important to the process and helped us not be bias towards our designs and ideas.
  • Mobile first design made it so simple for us to extrapolate the design into a desktop version, and saved us a lot of time overall.
